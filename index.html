Experience

Experience the best desktops ever created and choose the one you like most. Get a familiar interface so you can feel at home (or not), then turn that desktop into your dream desktop. You can even swap them as needed.

Freedom

Say goodbye to ads, forced updates, planned obsolecence, deceptive design, trackers, proprietary software, bloatware, "cloud" and everything else you don't want. Most Linux distros are free to use, as well as open source.

Apps

Find your old favourites, as well as exclusive software that is perfectly integrated with your desktop. If you still desperately need Windows software, you can dual-boot and keep both systems, without any compromises.

Community

Wide, helpful comunities are available to support you in installing, setting up, using, troubleshooting and mantaining your system. Your questions can be answered in as little as 24 hours. Most Linux desktop developers care about their users and closely listen to their feedback.

Stability

Your system won't break when you need it the most. Safely download software and update it from one place, all at once and when you want to. Easily make scheduled backups to go back in time. No antivirus needed.

Speed and lightness

Use your computer to its full potential. Linux is light on resources and runs on computers ten years old, while still alowing you to make use of your new one.

You can switch to Linux – today.
